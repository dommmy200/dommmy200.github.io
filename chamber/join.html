Membership Level Benefits

Gold Membership

Maximum Visibility: Prominent placement in all member directories (print and online) Priority listing in event marketing materials and website listings Opportunity to sponsor major events or programs

Enhanced Networking: Exclusive access to networking events with high-profile attendees Dedicated account manager for personalized support and guidance Invitations to board meetings or committee participation

Premium Resources: Discounts on all workshops, seminars, and training programs Access to exclusive webinars, reports, and industry data Free consultations with business advisors or legal counsel (limited)

Brand Recognition: Co-branding opportunities with the organization Logo placement on all marketing materials and website (premium spot) Public recognition for their contributions

Silver Membership

Increased Visibility: Featured placement in member directories Inclusion in event marketing materials and website listings Opportunity to sponsor mid-sized events or programs (discounted rates)

Valuable Networking: Access to a variety of networking events Dedicated member support team for assistance Invitations to committee meetings (optional)

Essential Resources: Discounts on select workshops, seminars, and training programs Access to industry reports and resources Free consultations with business advisors or legal counsel (limited time)

Brand Exposure: Logo placement on website and marketing materials (standard spot) Recognition in newsletters or publications (optional)

Bronze Membership

Basic Visibility: Listed in member directories Inclusion in general event marketing materials

Networking Opportunities: Access to select networking events (discounted rates) Connection with other members through online platforms

Introductory Resources: Discounted rates on a limited number of workshops or training programs Access to basic industry resources

Brand Awareness: Logo listed in member directory (basic placement)

Non Profit Membership

Discounted Membership Fees: Reduced membership fees to support their mission and work

Promotional Opportunities: Opportunity to showcase their work through chamber channels (website, newsletter) Free or discounted space at events (subject to availability)

Capacity Building Resources: Access to relevant workshops or training programs at reduced rates Connection to other non-profit organizations and community resources

Advocacy Support: Collaboration on advocacy efforts that benefit the non-profit sector Increased visibility and credibility within the community
